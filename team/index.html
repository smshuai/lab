BIO
Shimin Shuai 帅世民
Shimin was born in Jiujiang, Jiangxi, China. He received his BSc from Zhejiang Univ at 2014 and his PhD from the Univ of Toronto at 2019. Then he was a EIPOD fellow at the European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL) from 2020 to 2022. He will start his own lab at SUSTech from Spring 2022. He lives with two cats, Stella and Momo. He loves reading, gaming, traveling and other fun things.
